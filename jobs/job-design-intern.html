Design Intern (UI / Visual / Marketing Assets)

Location: Pune / Hybrid
Duration: Internship (6 Months preferred)
Experience: 0–1 Year
Start Date: Immediate / As mutually agreed

About Rebind

Rebind is a technology startup building solutions in the consumer trust, product authentication, and brand engagement space. We work closely with brands to help them connect directly with end consumers through digital verification and engagement platforms.

As a startup, Rebind offers interns a hands-on learning environment, where you will work on real product interfaces and brand assets used by customers and internal teams.

Role Overview

We are looking for a Design Intern who is enthusiastic about learning and building clean, practical designs for mobile apps, websites, and internal portals. This role also involves creating marketing and sales assets such as brochures, PDFs, and presentation materials.

This internship is open to freshers and early-career designers who are eager to learn, experiment, and grow in a fast-paced startup environment.

Key Responsibilities

Product & UI Design

  • Design user interfaces for mobile applications and web platforms
  • Create layouts for internal dashboards and portals
  • Ensure designs are simple, usable, and consistent

Brand & Marketing Assets

  • Create design assets for brochures, PDFs, pitch decks, and one-pagers
  • Design visuals for websites, social media, and internal communication
  • Maintain brand consistency across all materials

Collaboration & Iteration

  • Work closely with product managers, developers, and founders
  • Incorporate feedback and iterate on designs quickly
  • Assist in preparing assets for development handoff

Required Skills & Tools

  • Basic understanding of UI/UX principles
  • Hands-on experience with design tools such as:
    • Figma (preferred)
    • Adobe XD / Photoshop / Illustrator (any is fine)
  • Ability to create clean, structured designs
  • Willingness to learn and improve continuously

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • Experience: 0–1 year in design (internships, academic projects, or self-learning)
  • Freshers with strong interest in design are welcome
  • Curious, creative, and open to feedback
  • Comfortable learning new tools and processes
  • Ability to manage tasks independently with guidance
  • Good communication and collaboration skills

What You Will Learn

  • Designing real-world products used by customers
  • End-to-end design workflow: concept → iteration → delivery
  • Collaboration with engineering and product teams
  • Creating business-ready design and marketing assets
  • How design decisions impact user experience and business outcomes

How to Apply

Please share:

  • Your Resume
  • Portfolio / Dribbble / Behance / Figma link (academic or personal projects are acceptable)
