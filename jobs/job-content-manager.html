Content Manager (On-Ground & Social Media)

Location: Pune (On-site)
Start Date: Immediate / As agreed

Role Overview

Are you creative, confident, bold, and love interacting with people? We're looking for a dynamic Content Manager who can create engaging content, connect with people confidently, and build brand awareness in the market.

This role is perfect for someone who thrives both behind the camera and in front of it — someone who can walk up to strangers, spark conversations, and turn them into compelling content that grows our brand's reach and drives real business impact.

Key Responsibilities

Social Media Content

  • Create high-quality, engaging content across social media platforms
  • Plan and execute social media strategies to improve reach and engagement
  • Stay updated with the latest social media and content trends
  • Edit videos and optimize content for maximum reach and audience engagement

On-Ground & Video Content

  • Confidently present on camera for reels, interviews, and videos
  • Record on-ground/street content and interact with people to create market awareness
  • Confidently speak to strangers, conduct conversations/interviews, and build engaging content in public settings
  • Generate leads by meeting people, networking, and creating awareness while recording content in the market

Requirements

Skills & Qualities:

  • Strong command of English (written & spoken)
  • Bold, confident, and comfortable speaking with strangers and in public settings
  • Comfortable being on camera and recording street/public content
  • Good video editing and content creation skills
  • Strong understanding of social media platforms and audience engagement
  • Creative mindset with attention to detail
  • Must be proactive, energetic, and confident in meeting new people

Location:

Candidate should be available in Pune
