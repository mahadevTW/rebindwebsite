What exactly does Rebind-Trust do?

Rebind-Trust helps manufacturers create regulatory-compliant QR codes, track products across consumers, and enable consumer verification of authenticity—all through a single integrated platform.

Who should use Rebind-Trust?

Pharmaceutical manufacturers, food & beverage companies, medical device makers, and any company concerned with product authentication and supply chain compliance.

Will Rebind-Trust work with our existing printer?

Likely yes. We support Zebra, Domino, Markem-Imaj, and 20+ other industrial printer brands. Contact us for printer-specific compatibility verification.

How long does integration take?

Typical integration: 1-2 days. Complex custom requirements: 1-2 weeks. We provide full onboarding support and can expedite if needed.

Is Rebind-Trust CDSCO compliant?

Yes. We're specifically designed for CDSCO batch numbering, expiry tracking, and labeling requirements. All compliance documentation is automatically generated.

What support do you provide?

Starter: Email support (24-hour response). Professional: 24/7 phone/email. Enterprise: Dedicated account manager + 24/7 support.
