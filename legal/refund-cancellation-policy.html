Refund/Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

This Refund/Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancelling subscriptions and requesting refunds for Rebind-Trust services. By subscribing to our Service, you agree to this policy.

2. Subscription Cancellation

2.1 How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time through one of the following methods:

  • Log into your account and use the cancellation feature in account settings
  • Contact our support team at mahadev.kv@rebind.in
  • Call us at +91 90969 69220

2.2 When Cancellation Takes Effect

  • Immediate Cancellation: Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
  • End of Billing Period: You will continue to have access to all features until the subscription period expires
  • No Automatic Renewal: After cancellation, your subscription will not renew automatically

2.3 Data After Cancellation

After your subscription expires:

  • Your account will be downgraded to a free tier (if available) or deactivated
  • You may export your data for up to 30 days after cancellation
  • We may delete your account data after 90 days of inactivity, subject to legal retention requirements

3. Refund Policy

3.1 General Refund Terms

Rebind operates on a subscription-based model. Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Monthly Subscriptions: Generally non-refundable once payment is processed
  • Annual Subscriptions: Refund requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis within 14 days of initial purchase
  • Free Trial: No refund applicable as no payment is charged

3.2 Eligible Refund Scenarios

We may provide refunds in the following circumstances:

  • Service Failure: If we are unable to provide the Service due to technical issues on our end
  • Billing Error: If you were charged incorrectly due to our error
  • Duplicate Payment: If you were charged multiple times for the same subscription
  • Unauthorized Transaction: If your payment method was used without authorization
  • Annual Subscription (Early Cancellation): Pro-rated refund may be available if cancelled within 14 days of purchase, minus any usage fees

3.3 Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after subscription activation
  • Failure to use the Service or lack of understanding of features
  • Violation of Terms of Service resulting in account termination
  • Usage-based charges (e.g., QR codes generated, scans processed)
  • Third-party fees or charges
  • Refund requests made more than 14 days after purchase (for annual subscriptions)

4. Refund Process

4.1 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Contact our support team at mahadev.kv@rebind.in with your account details
  2. Provide the reason for your refund request
  3. Include your transaction ID or invoice number
  4. Allow 5-7 business days for review

4.2 Refund Review and Processing

  • Review Period: We will review your request within 5-7 business days
  • Approval: If approved, refunds will be processed within 10-15 business days
  • Refund Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used
  • Processing Time: The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your payment provider (typically 3-10 business days)

5. Pro-Rated Refunds

For annual subscriptions cancelled early (within 14 days), we may calculate a pro-rated refund based on:

  • The unused portion of your subscription period
  • Any setup fees or one-time charges (non-refundable)
  • Usage-based charges incurred during the active period

6. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider:

  • Your account may be immediately suspended pending resolution
  • We will provide transaction documentation to your payment provider
  • If the dispute is resolved in your favor, we will process the refund accordingly
  • We encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Service Discontinuation

If we discontinue the Service, we will provide pro-rated refunds for unused subscription periods.

7.2 Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure events beyond our control, refund policies may be adjusted, and we will communicate any changes to affected users.

8. Enterprise and Custom Plans

For Enterprise and custom subscription plans:

  • Refund terms are governed by the specific contract or agreement
  • Please refer to your signed agreement for cancellation and refund terms
  • Contact your account manager for assistance

9. Currency and Taxes

  • Refunds will be processed in the same currency as the original payment
  • Any currency conversion fees are the responsibility of the customer
  • Tax refunds, if applicable, are subject to local tax regulations

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund/Cancellation Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
